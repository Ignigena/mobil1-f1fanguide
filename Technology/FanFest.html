This Grand Prix celebration is a truly interactive in-person experience featuring 11 blocks of free activities in downtown Austin.

Technology Zone

Experience the technology
behind the racing.

The Mobil 1 Racing Experience. Feel, see and hear what it’s like to be in a Grand Prix race car. Let Vodafone McLaren Mercedes driver, Jenson Button, take you on a virtual hot lap of the Circuit of Americas.

Expertise Zone

Become a Mobil 1 virtual VIP
at races.

Go behind the scenes with the
Mobil 1 Virtual Garage, an interactive tour of the Vodafone McLaren Mercedes team’s garage at a Grand Prix or the Stewart-Haas Racing team’s garage at a NASCAR race. Explore the garage areas as a special VIP and find out what it takes to operate two of the most successful racing teams in the world.

Performance Zone

Discover the Mobil lubricants and fuels that power the Vodafone McLaren Mercedes team and how that technology is applied off the track.

Learn more about the technological benefits of Mobil 1™, Exxon and Mobil fuels and other solutions like Speedpass™ and the ExxonMobil Smart Card here at the Performance Zone.

Experience Zone

Have you got what it takes to be a Grand Prix driver or team member?
